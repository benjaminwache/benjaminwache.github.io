## About Me

 Hi! I am Benjamin Wache, a researcher in the International Economics section of [CPB Netherlands Bureau for Economic Policy Analysis (Centraal Planbureau)](https://www.cpb.nl/en). I hold a PhD in Economics from the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, and I am a Research Fellow of the [Research Group "International Finance and Geopolitics"](https://www.kielinstitut.de/institute/research-centers/international-finance/international-finance-and-geopolitics/) at the Kiel Institute.

## Research Interests

- International Economics (primary)
- Spatial Economics
- Finance
- Macroeconomics
